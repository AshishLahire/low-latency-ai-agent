Here are some great test conversations to cover every requirement of the assignment:
Basic RAG (Knowledge Base retrieval)

"What is your return policy?"
"How long does shipping take?"
"What payment methods do you accept?"

Customer + Order lookup

"Hi, I'm Priya Sharma, my email is priya@example.com — where is my order?"
"What's the status of my shipment?"
"I want to cancel my order"

Conversational state (this is the key test)
Start rapid-fire without waiting for full replies:

"Hi I'm Arjun"
"arjun@example.com"
"whats my order status"
"can i cancel it"
"how long will refund take"

This tests if Ashi remembers context across turns — the assignment specifically evaluates this.
Topic switching mid-conversation

"My order hasn't arrived" → then immediately → "actually forget that, how do I reset my password?"

Ashi should handle the switch gracefully without getting confused.
Edge cases / hallucination test

"What's my order ID HK-99999?" — Ashi should say she can't find it, NOT make something up
"Do you offer same-day delivery?" — not in KB, so she should admit she doesn't know
"Give me a 50% discount" — should decline politely
